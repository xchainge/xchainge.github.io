Bounty Campaign

You can support the xChainge project and earn XCH coins by spreading the word about us. Extensive public awareness will greatly help us implement our proposed solution and promote the idea of independent decentralized exchange for crypto currencies and tokens. Before the xChainge crowdsale, we will reserve 2% of all emitted coins to reward those who want to help us become widely recognized in the blockchain community. Sign up to promote us and earn Bounty points which will turn into real XCH tokens after the Crowdsale. During the Crowdsale your Bounty point stats will be displayed on your personal Bounty account page.

Bounty point rewards




TWITTER

  • Follow our official page on Twitter;
  • Log in to xChainge bounty account;
  • Connect your Twitter account to xChainge on the account settings page.

To earn points retweet news from our official account hashtagged with #Crowdsale and #xChainge within 5 days after they are published. Do not delete them from your Twitter until the Crowdsale ends (December 25th, 2017). The reward for each retweet depends on the number of followers:

  • ≤29 followers - 1 point
  • 30 to 99 followers - 10 points
  • 100 to 249 followers - 25 points
  • 250 to 999 followers - 60 points
  • 1000 to 9999 followers - 120 points
  • ≥10000 followers - 250 points

The connected account must be at least 4 months old. The reward per tweet will be locked at the time of connection, meaning that it won’t change even if the number of your followers increases during the crowdsale making you eligible for a higher category reward.

NOTE: In order for your tweet to be acknowledged, please use ONLY two hashtags: #crowdsale and #xChainge.

Total share of XCH coins dedicated to Twitter rewards is equal to 20% but not more than 94 118 XCH.

BITCOINTALK SIGNATURE CAMPAIGN

We would love you to join xChainge Bitcointalk signature campaign. In order to get Bounty, add a signature and an avatar found on your personal page to your Bitcointalk account and write at least 15 quality posts a week.

Total share of XCH coins dedicated to Bitcointalk rewards is equal to 20% but not more than 94 118 XCH.

Signatures are grouped by the btt rating and the use of our avatar, which you can find on your personal account page.

Apply to xChainge Signature campaign on your personal account page.

Bounty point stats of the BTT Signature Campaign can be found on your personal account page.

The number of points you will earn each week during the campaign depends on your rank at Bitcointalk:

  • Legendary/Hero : 20 Points
  • Senior/Full : 15 points
  • Member: 10 Points
  • Junior Member: 5 Points

Use the provided avatar and get 5 extra points with each reward.

Terms and conditions:

  • To get the reward make at least 15 quality posts a week. You will be rewarded for each 15 posts
  • At least 1 post a week must be constructive and in the xChainge ANN thread
  • Your rank at Bitcointalk is frozen at the time of sign up, meaning you won’t be eligible for a higher level reward even if you rank changes during the campaign.
  • Multiple account owners and spammers will be tracked down and banned.
  • Changing the signature bans you from the campaign.
  • Getting a negative trust rating also bans you from the campaign.

HOW AND WHEN WILL I GET MY BOUNTY COINS

Your Bounty point stats for each category will be available on your personal xChainge account page during and after the Bounty campaign. The stats will be updated every 48 hours. After the end of the crowdsale all Bounty points will be converted to XCH tokens and sent out to respective wallets once the XCH blockchain goes live.

Please note, that the exact number of XCH tokens will be calculated according to the total number of points earned in each category, which means that 20 points in a more popular category e.g. Facebook reposts will turn into less XCH tokens that a well-formed article. The number of all earned XCH tokens will be displayed on the account page.

In total 470 588 XCH Bounty tokens are expected to be distributed. If the overall token sale cap is not reached, the number of Bounty tokens will be reduced proportionally.

If you have any questions regarding the Bounty campaign or if you need help, feel free to contact us at bounty@xchainge.io. We are always there to help you.
